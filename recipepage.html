Chocolate Chip Cookies

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup butter
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 cup chocolate chips
  • 1 tsp baking soda

Instructions:

Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Cream together butter and sugar. Gradually add flour and baking soda. Stir in chocolate chips. Drop by spoonfuls onto a baking sheet and bake for 10-12 minutes.

Nutritional Information:

Nutrient Amount per Serving
Calories 150
Fat 8g
Carbohydrates 20g
Protein 2g
Sugar 10g
